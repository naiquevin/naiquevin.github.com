Music

I am not sure if the title of this page is appropriate. Calling myself a musician is like calling HTML a programming language! Anyway, I have been playing guitar since past 12 years. Although terribly out of touch at the moment, it's still significantly more than a hobby to me. These days since programming has taken a front seat, I only get time to practice at home and don't play with any bands or (home) record original stuff as I used to do when I was in college. I have a few videos uploaded to youtube though. Let me know if you like them :-)

My gear consists of an Ibanez RG350EX electric guitar, a Marshall 30dfx Combo Amplifier, MXR ZW-44 Distortion pedal, Jim Dunlop Crybaby and a Walden D350CE acoustic guitar.
